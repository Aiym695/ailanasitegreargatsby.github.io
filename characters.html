Jay Gatsby

A mysterious millionaire who throws extravagant parties to win back Daisy Buchanan. Represents the American Dream and the illusions behind it.

Daisy Buchanan

Beautiful and shallow. Torn between Gatsby and Tom. Symbolizes unattainable dreams and moral decay.

Nick Carraway

Narrator of the story. An honest observer from the Midwest, caught between illusion and reality.

Tom Buchanan

Rich, arrogant and aggressive. Represents the corruption and entitlement of old money.
